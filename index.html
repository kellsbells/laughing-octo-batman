I guess it all started when I wanted to write a blog and was trying to find a design. Nothing seemed totally right and I had ideas of my own I wanted to implement. So I started by deleting lines of code and seeing how it changed the page. That was pretty much the beginning of the end because I haven't stopped coding since, although now I try to take a little bit more of an educated approach.

After breaking down countless blogs I decided to take it a step further and applied and was accepted to DevMountain, an intensive, twelve week front end web development bootcamp. My experience there was mind-blowing. The world of web development totally opened up to me and I fell in love. Each lesson was incredibly fast paced and pretty much a whirlwind, it was amazing.
